Making Black Milk Tea with Brown Sugar Boba

  1. Gather all ingredients: 0.5lb bag of dried tapioca boba pearls, 0.5lb bag of loose leaf black tea, brown sugar, 0.25lb of powdered creamer, water.
  2. Boil an entire pot of water at medium heat
  3. Once it starts boiling, add an entire bag of dried tapioca boba pearls
  4. Stir every 10 minutes for 30 minutes, making sure the pearls don't stick to the bottom of the pot
  5. Once the 30 minutes are up, pour the pot over a large strainer. Rinse the pearls under cold water
  6. In a separate container, add 1.5 cups of cold water and 1 cup of brown sugar. Stir until sugar has dissolved
  7. Add the pearls into the container with the brown sugar syrup. Stir to incorporate completely.
  8. Set aside pearls for later. In another large pot, fill it up 3/4 with water to boil.
  9. Once the water starts to boil, add entire bag of loose black tea leaves into the pot. Cover the pot with the lid.
  10. Turn the heat down to medium. Wait 10 minutes for the tea to steep.
  11. After 10 minutes, turn off the heat and let it set for 5 more minutes. Then, scoop out the tea leaves with a large strainer as soon as the time is up to prevent bitterness.
  12. Add ice into the pot until the tea level reaches the top. Stir until the ice melts.
  13. Pour the tea into a separate large container. Add 24 oz powdered creamer and stir until thoroughly combined.
  14. Add one scoop of boba pearls into a cup. Then add desired amount of ice.
  15. Add desired amount of tea, followed by desired amount of sugar into the cup.
  16. Stir drink until fully incorporated.
  17. Sip and enjoy the milk tea with a boba straw!
